Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for tournaments?

Visit the respective game page (Free Fire or Valorant), fill out the registration form, complete the payment, and you're all set!

What's the registration fee?

The registration fee is ₹20 per team for all tournaments. This helps us maintain server costs and organize professional events.

How many players per team?

Free Fire: 4 main players + 2 substitutes. Valorant: 5 main players + 2 substitutes. Solo registrations are also available.

What are the prizes?

Prizes vary per tournament. Typically include cash prizes, gaming gear, in-game currency, and exclusive merchandise.

When will I get tournament details?

After successful registration and payment verification, you'll receive tournament details via email within 24 hours.

What's the refund policy?

Refunds are available up to 24 hours before tournament start. Contact support with your transaction ID for refund requests.
